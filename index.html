SUSHMA SWARAJ
Indian politician and a Supreme Court lawyer




A senior leader of Bharatiya Janata Party, Swaraj served as the Minister of External Affairs of India in the first Narendra Modi government.

She was the second woman to hold the office, after Indira Gandhi.

A brief history about Sushma Swaraj :
  • Sushma Swaraj was born on 14 February 1952 at Ambala cantonment to Shri Hardev Sharma and Shrimati Laxmi Devi.
  • She was an Indian politician and member of the Bharatiya Janata Party.
  • In 1977, at the age of 25 years, Sushma Swaraj became the youngest Cabinet Minister in India.
  • She was the Union Minister for External Affairs in the government of India.
  • Sushma Swaraj holds record of becoming the first woman spokesperson of a national-level political party.
  • Sushma Swaraj holds credit of becoming the first woman Chief Minister.
  • She is also the first woman Union Cabinet Minister.
  • Sushma She was a member of the National Executive of Janata Party for four years.
  • Sushma Swaraj passed away on 6 August 2019.
